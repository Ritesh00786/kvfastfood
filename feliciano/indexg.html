About KV

Fast Food

We are a Fast Food based in the K.V College, serving up tasty breakfasts, brunch, lunches, and private Party Orders for students with wine and of course lots of cake! We get all of our supplies from small businesses and prepare a simple, well-balanced menu using the best Irish ingredients we can find. Walk-ins are welcome at K.V College Fast Food, and reservations can be arranged for groups of 5 or more.
